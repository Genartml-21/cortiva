Common questions

Things worth asking.

Will the caller know they're talking to AI?
We're upfront with you, and we let you choose. Some clients prefer the agent introduces itself as an AI assistant. Others want it indistinguishable. Either is technically possible. Legally and ethically, in most jurisdictions, transparent disclosure is the safer choice, and we'll guide you on it.
How long does it take to launch an agent?
Typically four to six weeks from kickoff to a live, production-ready agent. Two weeks for discovery and design, two to three weeks for build, training, and integration, and a structured rollout with you in the room.
What if it makes a mistake on a call?
Every call is transcribed and reviewed in week one. Mistakes are caught, root-caused, and fixed. After the first month, the rate of needed corrections drops sharply. We design clear escalation triggers from day one. If the agent isn't certain, it hands off, never improvises.
Can it work with our existing phone number?
Yes. We port your number or set up forwarding so the caller experience is unchanged. Same number, faster pickup.
Where is the data stored? Is it secure?
Your data lives in encrypted infrastructure in the region you choose. Transcripts and recordings are yours, exportable any time, and never used to train shared models. We can sign NDAs and DPAs as standard.
The process

Four steps.
Four to six weeks.
Then it runs.

We don't sell software. We build the agent, train it on your business, integrate it into your stack, and run it alongside your team. Here's how that actually goes, week by week.

01 · WEEK 1

Discovery

We sit with your team. Listen to a sample of real calls. Map the conversations that matter. Decide what the agent should and should not do. Output: a clear scope, a voice persona, a flow map.

02 · WEEKS 2–3

Build & train

We build the agent. Train it on your knowledge base, your pricing, your edge cases. Pick the voice. Hook in your CRM and calendar. You get a private test number to call yourself, as often as you want.

03 · WEEK 4

Soft launch

Live on 10–20% of calls. We watch every transcript in real time. Fix anything off. Add anything missing. By end of week, the agent is handling 100% of the calls it was designed for.

04 · ONGOING

Tune & grow

Weekly review of transcripts. Monthly performance report. Quarterly strategy. The agent gets sharper, the integrations get deeper, the load it carries grows. You scale without hiring.

Under the hood, briefly

What runs your agent.

Cortiva is built on a custom orchestration layer that ties together best-in-class speech, language, and voice models. We pick the right tool for each job rather than locking you into one vendor.

Speech understanding

Custom-trained recognition for Indian and global languages. Handles accents, background noise, and code-switching mid-sentence.

Reasoning engine

We orchestrate multiple language models so the agent picks the right one for the right moment. Cheap models for simple replies, deep models for hard decisions.

Voice synthesis

Premium voices that pass for human in most contexts. Tuned for your brand: warm, formal, brisk, regional, whatever fits.

Your data, your control

Hosted in the region you choose. Transcripts and recordings exportable any time. Never used to train shared models. NDAs and DPAs as standard.
